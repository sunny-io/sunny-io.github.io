Original Teamwork Project

CoWork2Go started out as the final project of my UX/UI-Design-Training. Our team, consisting of Herbert Sonnenschein, Ulyana Fidan and me, worked an idea how to make Coworking easier for people who didn't use it often or were traveling a lot.

Starting out with interviews, we identified user needs, e.g. "be sure there is a space open when I get there" and designed an App in Adobe XD, first as wireframe, later as actual prototype.

Result of the work as a team was a presentation (in German) and a video showing the prototype .

App-Abstract

  • Target Group: Individual End Users
  • Platform: Mobile Phone
  • Main Task: Booking a desk at at Coworking space

Backend Abstract

  • Target Group: Company employeed, Admins or Project Leads
  • Platform: Laptop/Desktop
  • Main Task: Setting up access for users to company billing of CoWork2Go bookings
