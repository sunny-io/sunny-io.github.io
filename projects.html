Original Teamwork Project

CoWork2Go was the final project of my UX/UI-Design-Training. Our team, consisting of Herbert Sonnenschein, Ulyana Fidan and me, developed an app concept to make Coworking easier for people who didn't use every day or were traveling a lot.

Starting out with interviews, we identified user needs, e.g. "be sure there is a space open when I get there". We did some paper-prototyping and testing. With the first test results, we designed an app in Adobe XD, first as wireframe, later as actual prototype.

Result of the work as a team was a presentation (in German) and a video showing the prototype .

App-Abstract

  • Target Group: Individual End Users
  • Primary plattform: Mobile Phone
  • Main Task: Booking a desk at at Coworking space

Methods

  • Prospective user interviews
  • User tests with Paper Prototypes
  • Personas
  • User Szenarios
  • Mood Board
  • XD-Prototype
